About me

I've been exploring new materials and mediums such as coding, animation, and pen tools in Adobe Illustrator, which has expanded my creative approach and helped me better understand the kind of art I want to create. My inspiration comes from the games I've played, the movies I've watched, and the cartoons I love. Reinterpreting these influences through my own visual language has been both motivating and exciting. My work is driven by a desire to create colorful, playful, and unpredictable pieces that reflect my interest in building imaginative and lively visual worlds.

Artist statement

My work explores identity, memory, and speculative worlds through digital illustration and mixed-media compositing. I build layered images that combine drawn elements, 3D forms, and photo textures to create scenes that feel cinematic and emotionally open.

Process is central to my practice: I sketch by hand, iterate digitally, and refine color and light to shape narrative mood. Each project is an invitation to pause, imagine, and enter spaces where personal and virtual histories overlap.
