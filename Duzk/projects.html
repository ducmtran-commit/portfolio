Sunset Aurora

Glitch collage album cover, 2026.

This project was about creating an album cover using glitch images. I chose the glitched sunset because it had the perfect balance for an album cover - the top half preserves the original sunset, while the bottom half contrasts with a photographic negative-like effect. The image is also split almost perfectly across the middle, which adds to its visual impact. Additionally, the sunset aesthetic fits well with the music style of Aurora, and the glitched effect even contains colors that resemble an aurora palette.
