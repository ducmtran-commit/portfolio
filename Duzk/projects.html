Vector Art

Vector Art — Sticker Design

Adobe Illustrator Pen Tool, 2026.

This project was about creating a sticker using vector art. I used the Pen Tool in Adobe Illustrator to design it. The sticker is based on my Yuumi cat figure from the game League of Legends. For this project, I tried to capture as many details of the figure as possible while ignoring the base that the figure normally sits on.

Vector Art — Mockup

Sticker application preview, 2026.

A presentation mockup showing how the final sticker design looks when applied to real surfaces.
